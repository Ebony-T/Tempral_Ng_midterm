YOU ARE WHAT YOU EAT

Why Eat Junk Food?

Benefits

  • It gives you a dopamine release.
  • It helps satisfy cravings.
  • It’s convenient and easily accessible.
  • It's affordable.
  • It tastes really good.

Healthy food is very nourishing but it’s important that everyone indulges whener they can because contrary to popular belief, there are many benefits to eating junk food. Specifically, its helpful for one’s mental health. Junk food can bring a great deal of comfort to those going through a hard time, or a great way to treat oneself when celebrating achievements. Coupling this with its general convenience and affordability, junk food is the way to go!

Why Eat Healthy Foods?

Benefits

  • It protects you against many chronic noncommunicable diseases, such as heart disease, diabetes, and cancer.
  • Helps the digestive system function.
  • Helps achieve and maintain a healthy weight.
  • May also boost your mood and give you more energy by maintaining a balanced diet.

Healthy foods keep your body in balance and on track. They help you manage your weight as well as live a healthier, youthful lifestyle. Junk food may be quite convenient, readily available on the go, and cheap, whereas healthy food is best for maintaining weight, getting an adequate amount of essential nutrients, and keeping you in good health. When you consume a diet packed with fresh natural produce, it helps lower the risk of several chronic disorders.
